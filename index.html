    Drill Y-42

    Project Introduction:

    Drill Y-42 is a steampunk themed endless runner, where the player drills through the earth to the next safe cave. Gathering resources, knowledge and tool upgrades in small tunnels found along their way.

    During the development I collaborated with the art department and a designated engineer!

    Download Drill Y-42

    DEVELOPMENT INFO: TASK OVERVIEW:
    Engine: Unity 4
    Genre: Endless Runner
    Dev. Time: 10 Weeks
    Team Size: 9
  • Created a level design for a procedural generation
  • Playtested and iterated
    		•
    MY CONTRIBUTION:
  • Prototyped a whitebox level for proof of concept and early play test
  • Designed and built modular tunnel segments, for a procedurally generated cave system
    • Designed varients of segments with different shapes, sizes, slopes and layouts to reduce repetition and improve exploration variety.
    • Created modular level segments with varied layouts, designed to support both gameplay flow and environmental diversity
    • Maintained seamless connectivity between segments within the modular generation system
    • Iterated lighting within level segments to enhance atmosphere, readability, and overall player experience
  • Designed planned biomes for player and story progression and environmental variation
    • Focused on clear differences in color, sound and set dressing
    • Planned mechanic, tool, upgrade and resource unlock
  • Balanced player movement ⇒ walking, running, jumping
  • Worked with Unity Tools to create and iterate a modular level ⇒ ProBuilder, PolyBrush
  • Worked with Confluence and Jira, for documentation and task tracking

    Power of Goo

    Project Introduction:

    Power of Goo is a 2D Pixel Art Platformer / Metroidvania in which the player progresses through various levels, facing different obstacles, be it environmental or a hazardous enemy. The player will obtain new abilities throughout the playthrough which they will need to access new areas.

    During the development I collaborated with the art- and engineering department, to create this project

    Download Power of Goo

    DEVELOPMENT INFO: TASK OVERVIEW:
    Engine: Godot
    Genre: Metroidvania
    Dev. Time: 10 Weeks
    Team Size: 6
  • Created the Game- and Narrative Design
  • Coordinated the development progress, supported team members and resolve conflicts
  • Designed UI/UX and worked on SFX
    		•
    MY CONTRIBUTION:
  • Designed and prototyping player movement ⇒ walk, jump, double jump, crawl
  • Designed and playstested combat system
    • Designed a two-step combat system, where enemies must be stunned to be damaged ⇒ spit, punch
    • Designed two enemies with unique combat, visual, sound and behaviour
    • Designed a boss fight, with two separate phases and built the boss arena
  • Designed a tutorial with focus on a visual showcasing
  • Designed and Implemented UI/UX
    • Researched UI References for clear vision
    • Designed Wireframe and Flowchart for visualization
    • Implented the UI into engine
  • Researched, edited and combined SFX and music snippets to match the game's audio direction
  • Wrote the intro's narrative, designed its visual direction, directed the voice-over and edited the final audio with music and ambient sound
