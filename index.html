    Overview

    Power of Goo is a short 2D Pixel Art Metroidvania built in Godot, which the player progresses through various levels, facing different obstacles and enemies.

    The player will obtain new abilities, to access new areas, rescue the other slimes and deafeat this enemy filled underground.
